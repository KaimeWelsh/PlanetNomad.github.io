About Us

Welcome to Planet Nomad, an environmentalist store/vlog dedicated to helping the environment. We are raising money for a trip across the United States and Canada to learn more about the diversity and beauty of life in this country and others. Check out our store to buy original photographs and artwork, or check out our vlogs for videos on everything from endagered animals to vegan recipies. You can help keep up going by going to our "gofundme" page and donating!
